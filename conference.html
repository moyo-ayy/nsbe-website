2025 Annual NSBE Convention

Date: March 5-9, 2025

Location: McCormick Place, Chicago, IL

The 2025 annual convention is a four-day event with a over 400 Career fair exhibitors and 15,000 attendees. During the convention, the NSBE community conducts organizational business, elects national and regional leaders, holds competitions, provides professional and personal development and networking and celebratory activities.

Why?

The NSBE National Convention is a prime opportunity for students to network, secure internships, and gain industry insights. Attendees can connect directly with top companies, attend career-advancing workshops, compete in hackathons, and access scholarships. Overall, the event fosters a dynamic and supportive environment, professional growth opportunities, and a strong sense of community.

How do I verify my Academic Pyramid of Excellence Designation (APEx) status?

1. To be an APEx member you must have at least a 3.0 GPA.
2. Before proceeding, please verify that you renewed/activated your NSBE member for the year.
3. Self report your GPA on your NSBE profile and fill out the GPA collection form.
4. Have the form signed by the Office of Registrar and e-mail the form to scholarships@nsbe.org.
** Note: Your GPA must be verified semseter while your APEx status is verified every year. **
