Our Mission

To increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers, who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) with more than 19,000 members is the largest student-managed organization in the country. NSBE's mission is to increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community. NSBE is comprised of more than 270 chapters on college and university campuses, 75 Alumni Extension chapters nationwide and 75 Pre-College chapters. These chapters are geographically divided into six regions.
